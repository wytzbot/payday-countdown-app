Privacy Policy for Payday Countdown

Last updated: 2026-07-06

Data Collection

Payday Countdown does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data. All information you enter (salary, balance, expenses) is stored locally on your device using localStorage. It never leaves your phone.

Third Parties

We use no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting, and no third-party trackers. The app loads TailwindCSS and Alpine.js from public CDNs only.

Permissions

Notifications: Optional. Used only to send daily budget reminders from your device. You can disable anytime in browser/OS settings.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13. It is a general audience utility.

Financial Disclaimer

This app is a calculator for educational purposes only. It is not financial advice. For debt, investment, or tax decisions, consult a licensed financial advisor.

Questions: support@yourdomain.com

Changes
